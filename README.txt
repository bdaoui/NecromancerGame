NECROMANCER
This is a simple adventure/incrementer game on plain JavaScript. Help the necromancer kill the king by any mean.

Mechanics
The necromancer can summon the dead, different types of dead are good at different tasks:
1) Zombie Animals can hunt down Villagers;
2) Zombie Villagers can use fire to defeat Trolls, but are too slow to catch Villagers;
3) Zombie Trolls can breach the castle defenses.

Enemies also have different ways to interact with you:
1) Animals are harmless;
2) Villagers kill Animals;
3) Troll kill Villagers and then your other Zombies. Then, you. 
4) The King can and will attack all your horde.
5) Dragons are crazy.


Have fun.
